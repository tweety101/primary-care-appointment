Comprehensive Primary Care in Your Neighborhood

Health Quest Medical Practice (HQMP) offers high-quality, personalized primary care for infants, children, adolescents and adults at convenient locations throughout the Mid-Hudson Valley and northwestern Connecticut.

Our primary care providers include specialists in pediatrics, family medicine and internal medicine. When you choose HQMP, you’ll have access to the resources of the Health Quest family of award-winning hospitals and healthcare providers. So if you or your family require more specialized care, your provider can refer you to an HQMP specialist and still remain a key partner on your healthcare team.
